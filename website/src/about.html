Mission

Why it exists

In my personal and professional life, I use multiple Google accounts for Gmail, Google Drive, Search, Maps, and many other services. I don't always use the same account for each service, so I often have to switch between accounts depending on which service I visit. This project was started as a scripting experiment, and extended to a project that has benefit to those managing multiple Google accounts for different Google services.

Built with Trust

The codebase embraces an open-source content policy for transparency and collaboration. Every line of code is auditable.

Tech Stack

Built purely with HTML, CSS, & JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies, just clean vanilla code.

Privacy First

Everything lives in local storage. No sync, no external servers, no tracking. Your data stays on your machine.
